## Important Concepts Quick Reference Guide

This guide includes commonly used professional terms and important concepts in advertising systems to help you quickly understand and reference.

### Core Concepts

**CTR (Click-Through Rate)**
- Definition: The number of times an ad is clicked divided by the number of times it is displayed
- Formula: CTR = (Clicks / Impressions) × 100%
- Purpose: Measure ad appeal and relevance

**CPC (Cost Per Click)**
- Definition: The amount an advertiser pays for each click
- Formula: CPC = Total Cost / Number of Clicks
- Purpose: Evaluate ad placement costs

**CPM (Cost Per Mille)**
- Definition: The amount an advertiser pays for every 1,000 impressions
- Formula: CPM = (Total Cost / Impressions) × 1,000
- Purpose: Evaluate ad display costs

**ROI (Return on Investment)**
- Definition: The ratio of returns to costs from advertising investment
- Formula: ROI = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100%
- Purpose: Evaluate ad placement effectiveness

### Ad Types

**Display Ads**
- Ads displayed in image, text, or video format
- Typically appear on websites, apps, and other media platforms

**Search Ads**
- Ads displayed on search results pages
- Usually related to user search keywords

**Native Ads**
- Ads that match the format of media content
- Blend into user experience and are less recognizable as ads

### Placement Strategies

**Targeting**
- Precisely deliver ads based on user characteristics, interests, behaviors, etc.
- Improve ad relevance and conversion rates

**Bidding Strategy**
- Advertisers set bidding methods to obtain ad display opportunities
- Common strategies: CPC, CPM, CPA, etc.
